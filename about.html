My Testimony

Healed to Heal Others

My name is Natalie Guerro, and I am an intuitive faith healer. My journey to this calling began in the quiet shadows of my youth. Growing up I was a shy, nervous child who often sought isolation to cope with a deep, persistent sadness.

Despite the heaviness I carried, I was never truly alone. I felt a constant, thinning veil between me and the divine—a connection to my ancestors, the Archangels, and God. In my most restless nights, I would talk to God until the sun rose or sleep finally took me.

Between 2020 and 2023, life began to feel incredibly heavy. Desperate to heal my sadness, I began searching for spiritual relief. This led me to explore tarot, reiki, and mediumship, hoping to find the “missing piece.”

"I even enrolled in classes to deepen my understanding, but with every certificate earned, I was met with the same hollow echo. I was learning the tools, but I wasn't finding the cure."

By May 2023, I reached a point of total exhaustion. Defeated and hopeless, I fell to my knees and cried out to God, begging for guidance and a sense of peace.

I arrived at my appointment trembling with nerves and fear, but I walked out a different person. For the first time in my life, the heavy sadness that had defined me was simply gone; I felt free, relieved, and remarkably light.

Today, I stand as an instrument of the Divine, dedicated helping others break through their own shadows to find the freedom and peace that only true faith can provide.

"If you feel like you are drowning in shadows, look for me. I am the proof that the morning always comes, and the light always wins."
