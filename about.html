Natalie Guerrero

Intuitive Faith Healer

Growing up, I was a very shy, nervous person who often isolated myself in quiet reflection. For the longest time, I suffered from severe sadness that eventually manifested as panic attacks and anxiety.

"I remember crying out to God almost down on my knees asking for guidance or to just feel at peace."

I always felt a deep connection to my ancestors, my Guardian angel, Archangels, and God. However, as I got older, I started to drift apart. It wasn't until 2020-2023 when everything started to feel very heavy that I began my search for true restoration.

After exploring many paths that left me feeling empty, I finally found a place of spiritual healing in May 2023. The sadness vanished. I felt free, relieved, and lighter. My faith has been restored, and I realized this was my calling: to be an instrument of God to help those in need.
