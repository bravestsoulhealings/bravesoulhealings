My Testimony

Healed to Heal Others

My name is Natalie Guerrero. I am an intuitive faith healer. Growing up, I was a very shy, nervous person who often isolated myself. For a long time, I carried a heavy weight of sadness that eventually manifested as panic attacks and anxiety.

I always felt a deep, innate connection to my ancestors, my Guardian angel, the Archangels, and God. However, as I grew older, I started to drift apart. It wasn't until 2020-2023 when the world felt its heaviest that I knew I needed to return home to my faith.

"I remember crying out to God, almost down on my knees, asking for guidance or to just feel at peace."

I searched for natural ways to heal—exploring many paths—but every time I finished a class, I still felt empty. In May 2023, feeling hopeless, I found a place of spiritual healing that was different. When I arrived, the sadness I always felt vanished. I felt free, relieved, and lighter.

This inspired me to start my own healing journey. My faith has been restored, and I realized this was my calling: to be an instrument of God to help those in need.
