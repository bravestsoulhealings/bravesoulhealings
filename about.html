My Testimony

Healed to Heal Others

My name is Natalie Guerro, and I am an intuitive faith healer. My journey to this calling began in the quiet shadows of my youth. Growing up I was a shy, nervous child who often sought isolation to cope with a deep, persistent sadness. Despite the heaviness I carried, I was never truly alone. I felt a constant, thinning veil between me and the divine—a connection to my ancestors, the Archangels, and God.

In my most restless nights, I would talk to God until the sun rose or sleep finally took me. As I grew older, I drifted from the Catholic faith of my youth. Between 2020 and 2023, life began to feel incredibly heavy. Desperate to heal my sadness and fearful of traditional medication, I began searching for natural remedies and spiritual relief.

This search led me to explore tarot, reiki, and mediumship hoping to find the “missing piece”. I even enrolled in classes to deepen my understanding, but with every certificate earned and every course completed, I was met with the same hollow echo. I was learning the tools, but I wasn't finding the cure.

By May 2023, I reached a point of total exhaustion. Defeated and hopeless, I fell to my knees and cried out to God, begging for guidance and a sense of peace. In that moment of divine surrender, I was led to a spiritual healing that felt different from anything I had ever seen.

I arrived at my appointment trembling with nerves and fear, but I walked out a different person. For the first time in my life, the heavy sadness that had defined me was simply gone; I felt free, relieved, and remarkably light. This experience did more than just heal me; it restored my faith, led me to my tribe, and revealed my true calling. Today, I stand as an instrument of the Divine, dedicated helping others break through their own shadows to find the freedom and peace that only true faith can provide.
